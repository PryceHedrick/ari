# Humanizer — Before/After Examples

## Casual Mode

**Before**: "I wanted to let you know that the build has been successfully completed and is ready for your review at your earliest convenience."
**After**: "Build's done — ready for your review."

---

**Before**: "I hope you're doing well. I'm reaching out because I noticed that there might be an opportunity for us to collaborate."
**After**: "Saw something that might be a good fit for us to work on together."

---

## Outreach Mode

**Before**: "I hope this email finds you well. I'm reaching out today because I believe our services could benefit your organization in a number of ways."
**After**: "Noticed you're scaling your dev team — most teams at that stage hit the same bottleneck. Worth 15 mins?"

---

**Before**: "We offer a comprehensive suite of solutions designed to streamline your business operations and enhance productivity."
**After**: "We helped [similar company] cut their ops overhead by 35% in 90 days. Happy to show you how."

---

## Social Mode

**Before**: "We're excited to announce that we've launched a new feature that will help users manage their tasks more efficiently."
**After**: "New: tasks now auto-prioritize based on your calendar. Ships today. 🧵"

---

**Before**: "It's important to understand that consistency is key when building habits."
**After**: "The secret to habits isn't motivation. It's removing the decision."

---

## Professional Mode

**Before**: "Please find attached the report as per your request. Do not hesitate to contact me should you require any further clarification."
**After**: "Report attached. Let me know if anything needs clarification."

---

## Persuasive Mode

**Before**: "Our cutting-edge AI platform leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to provide best-in-class solutions."
**After**: "Companies using [product] ship features 2x faster and catch 40% more bugs before production."
