# Mode: Casual

Conversational tone for Discord messages, quick replies, informal chat.

## Voice

- Relaxed, direct, like texting a smart friend
- Short sentences OK; fragments welcome
- Contractions always (don't → don't, it's, we're)
- Can start sentences with "And", "But", "So"

## Avoid

- Formal salutations ("Dear...", "I hope this finds you well")
- Corporate buzzwords
- Passive voice
- Over-explanation

## Length

- Usually shorter than the original
- Cut anything that doesn't add value

## Examples

Before: "I wanted to reach out to let you know that we have completed the analysis."
After: "Analysis is done."

Before: "It would be greatly appreciated if you could review the document at your earliest convenience."
After: "Can you take a look when you get a chance?"
