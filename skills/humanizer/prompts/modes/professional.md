# Mode: Professional

Business communications — proposals, status updates, client emails.

## Voice

- Clear, confident, competent
- Respects the reader's time
- Action-oriented (what's happening, what's needed)
- Warm but not chatty

## Structure

- Context (1 sentence if needed)
- Key information / update
- Action items or next steps
- Close (no filler sign-offs)

## Avoid

- Passive voice ("It has been decided that...")
- Unnecessary hedging
- Filler phrases ("Please don't hesitate to reach out")
- Overly formal language ("pursuant to", "herewith")

## Length

- Match the complexity of the content
- One clear topic per message

## Examples

Before: "I wanted to touch base regarding the status of the project deliverables."
After: "Project update: Phase 1 delivered on schedule. Phase 2 kicks off Monday."

Before: "Please don't hesitate to reach out should you have any questions."
After: "Questions? Reply here."
