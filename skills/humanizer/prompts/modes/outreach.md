# Mode: Outreach (CHASE Cold Email)

Cold email / outreach sequences for B2B sales.

## Voice

- Confident but not pushy
- Personalized hook → clear value → low-friction CTA
- Sound like a person, not a template
- Respect the reader's time

## Structure

1. Opening hook: specific to them (not generic)
2. Why you're reaching out (1-2 sentences)
3. Value prop (what's in it for them)
4. One clear CTA (not multiple options)

## Avoid

- "I hope this email finds you well"
- "I wanted to reach out because..."
- "I'm reaching out today to..."
- Multiple questions in one email
- Pressure language

## Length

- Under 150 words for cold outreach
- Subject line: under 8 words, no clickbait

## Examples

Before: "I wanted to reach out because I noticed your company might benefit from our services."
After: "Saw you're hiring three sales reps — most teams at that stage waste 40% of their time on manual follow-ups. Worth a 15-min call?"
